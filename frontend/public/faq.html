Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is RoadRescue360?

RoadRescue360 is a comprehensive roadside assistance service that provides 24/7 support for various vehicle-related emergencies. We offer services including battery service, fuel delivery, mechanical help, towing, lockout assistance, tire changes, mobile repairs, and accident recovery.

How do I request roadside assistance?

You can request assistance through our website by clicking the "Schedule Service" button, calling our emergency hotline, or using our mobile app. We'll dispatch the nearest available service provider to your location.

What areas do you service?

We service all major metropolitan areas and surrounding suburbs. Our service coverage includes emergency roadside assistance within a 50-mile radius of our service centers. Contact us to confirm coverage in your specific area.

Service Details

How long does it take for help to arrive?

Our average response time is 30-45 minutes, depending on your location and traffic conditions. For emergency situations, we prioritize faster response times and aim to arrive within 20-30 minutes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital payment methods. Some services may be covered by insurance or roadside assistance programs. Contact us for specific payment details.

Do you offer membership plans?

Yes, we offer various membership plans that provide discounted rates and priority service. Members also receive additional benefits like free towing and battery service. Visit our membership page for detailed information.

Technical Questions

What types of vehicles do you service?

We service all types of vehicles including cars, trucks, SUVs, motorcycles, and RVs. Our technicians are certified to work on various makes and models. Contact us for specific vehicle compatibility information.

Do you provide parts and supplies?

Yes, we carry a wide range of common parts and supplies for emergency repairs. For specialized parts, we can recommend local auto parts stores or order them for you.

What happens if you can't fix my vehicle?

If we cannot repair your vehicle on-site, we'll arrange for towing to a nearby repair shop of your choice or recommend trusted repair facilities in your area.
