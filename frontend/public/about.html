About RoadRescue360

We're on a mission to revolutionize roadside assistance with fast, reliable, and transparent service

Our Story

Founded in 2018, RoadRescue360 began with a simple idea: roadside assistance should be fast, reliable, and stress-free. After experiencing the frustration of long wait times and unreliable service firsthand, our founder Alex Chen decided there had to be a better way.

Starting with just three vehicles and a small team in San Francisco, we've now expanded to serve major cities across the country with a fleet of over 200 vehicles and 500+ certified technicians. Our growth has been fueled by our commitment to exceptional service and cutting-edge technology.

Today, RoadRescue360 is recognized as an industry leader, handling over 10,000 roadside assistance calls monthly with an average response time of under 30 minutes. We continue to innovate and expand our services to ensure drivers everywhere have access to help when they need it most.

Our Mission & Values

Speed

We understand that when you're stranded, every minute counts. Our goal is to reach you as quickly as possible with an average response time under 30 minutes.

Reliability

We arrive when we say we will. Our service is consistently dependable with real-time tracking so you're never left wondering when help will arrive.

Quality

All our technicians are certified professionals with extensive training and experience. We invest in the best tools and equipment to solve your problem right the first time.

Compassion

We treat every customer with empathy and respect. Being stranded is stressful, and we're committed to making the experience as comfortable as possible.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through fuel-efficient vehicles, optimized routing, and eco-friendly practices throughout our operations.

Innovation

We continuously improve our technology and processes to provide better, faster service. From our app to our dispatch systems, we embrace innovation at every level.
